Sobre

For over 20 years, Bucci Atleir has been a full-service bridal salon serving in Nova Iguacu area. Special moments are created here. We've helped thousands of brides look and feel beautiful for their big days. From finding the perfect wedding dress to styling bridesmaid groups, we’re here to bring bridal visions to life. We’d love to help in any way we can! We make exquisite wedding dresses that are exclusive to our customer needs.
